How to Make a Cup of Coffee!

What You Will Need:

  • A coffee maker
  • Water
  • A coffee filter
  • Coffee grounds
  • A coffee cup

Just a Few Simple Steps:

  1. Fill your coffee maker with your desired amount of water.
  2. Place your coffee filter in the filter tray.
  3. Fill your coffee filter with your desired amount of coffee grounds. This should be proportional to the amount of water you're using.
  4. Press the "brew" button and wait for your coffee pot to fill up.
  5. Enjoy your fresh cup of joe!
