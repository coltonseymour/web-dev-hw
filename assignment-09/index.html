HOW TO MAKE THE PERFECT CUP OF COFFEE!



Just A Few Simple Steps:



Step 1 - Find What You Will Need

Items:

  • A coffee maker
  • Water
  • A coffee filter
  • Coffee grounds
  • A coffee cup

  • Optional: Coffee creamer




Step 2 - Prepare Your Coffee Maker

Fill your coffee pot with water equalling your desired ammount of coffee (see the cup markings on the side of your foccee pot). Then, pour it into the water reservoir, typically located in the back of your coffee maker.

After that, place your coffee filter in the filter tray. Fill it with your coffee grounds corresponding to the amount of water that you are using.

Step 3 - Time To Brew!

Hit the "brew" button on your coffee maker and watch it drip down into the pot.

As soon as it finishes, pour yourself a fresh, hot cup of joe. Add creamer, or just drink it black!
